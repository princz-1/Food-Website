Amm Panna(Sisuda) With Recipe

To make aam panna, boil 2 large raw mangoes until soft, then peel and extract the pulp. Blend the pulp with 1 cup sugar, 1 tsp black salt, 1 tsp roasted cumin powder, and 1/2 cup mint leaves until smooth. Transfer to a pitcher and add 4 cups of water, adjusting sweetness and saltiness to taste. Chill for 1-2 hours. Serve over ice cubes in glasses, garnished with mint leaves if desired. Aam panna is a refreshing summer drink, rich in tangy mango flavor with hints of mint and cumin, perfect for beating the heat. Enjoy this traditional Indian beverage!
